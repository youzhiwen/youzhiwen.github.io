Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using the Knowing Body App.

Information We Collect

We collect data to improve the app and enhance your learning experience. This includes:

  • Device Information: Type of device, OS version, unique identifiers, and general system data
  • Usage Statistics: Pages viewed, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Language Preferences: Language you select to view content

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Provide technical support if needed

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent any personal data.

Your information may be shared only under the following conditions:

  • With your consent: When you allow us to share information
  • Third-party services: Only for backend services such as analytics and crash reporting
  • Legal obligations: If required by law

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure transmission protocols (e.g., HTTPS)
  • Storage encryption and access controls
  • Regular code and infrastructure audits
  • Separation of sensitive and anonymous data

While we aim to ensure top security, no method is 100% secure over the internet.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed:

  • Usage data: 12 to 24 months
  • Crash reports: Up to 12 months
  • Device data: While the app is installed
  • Any account-specific data (if applicable): Until user requests deletion

Your Rights and Choices

You can request the following:
